I have been a working artist for over fourteen years and teaching for the last eight. I work in a variety of mediums but lately have been working in Acrylic Paint. I teach High School Advanced Drawing in Austin, Texas as well as Art 1 and AP Drawing Portfolio.

I grew up in the Houston area and relocated to Austin Texas in 2000 where I attended UT for Studio Art. Since graduating in 2004, I have put my transmedia skills to work as a cheif editor for a web based television program in Houston, and worked on a number of small local productions. I began teaching in 2008 and love the people I have met along the way.

Most recently, I was the featured artist for Blue Moon Inc. at Euphoria Fest.
